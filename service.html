Service

I'm invested in building community in academia and supporting students navigating undergrad and grad school. Most of my service work focuses on mentorship, open science, and making psychology more accessible.

I currently serve as Co-President of the mentorship program Access Grads, where I previously worked as Media & Recruitment Coordinator from 2022-2025. I've been mentoring through Access Grads since 2021, and also mentor new teaching assistants through the Teaching Assistant Advisory Program (TAAP).

I'm a committee member on the Social Affective Neuroscience Society's Equity, Diversity, Inclusion, and Justice Committee, and previously served on the UCSB PBS Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Committee from 2022-2024 and the UCSB PBS Admissions Committee for the 2023 cycle.

I'm a founding member and organizer of the ReproducibiliTEA UCSB chapter, a journal club focused on open science and reproducibility. During my master's at the University of Amsterdam, I served as President for Cognito, the Brain and Cognitive Science student union.
