Research & Publications

My dissertation tests a computational model of how relational and cultural contexts influence emotional responses and moral judgment. In other work, I use neuroimaging and behavioral methods to understand how loneliness alters the way we represent our own and others' emotional states. I'm also interested in the emotions that sustain and hinder long-term mobilization in collective action.
